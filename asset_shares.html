Gold as % of global equity market cap — 1975 to 2025

Gold mcap as % of global equity market cap · 1975–2025
Source: World Bank CM.MKT.LCAP.CD + WFE • gold: WGC + LBMA

Denominator: global equity market capitalization. 4–20% band since 1990. ~3.9% at dot-com peak (2000); ~18% at commodity peak (2012); ~14.97% end-2025; 19.9% at 2026-05-14 spot.

Gold as % of total global financial assets — 1990 to 2025

Gold mcap as % of total global financial assets · 1990–2025
Source: FSB Global Monitoring Report (2014+); pre-2014 interpolated vs IMF GFSR / BIS

FSB definition: banks + insurance + pension funds + other financial intermediaries + public financial institutions; ~$487T end-2023. Includes bank balance sheets — a broader base than equities+bonds alone. Gold has moved from <1% (2000 equity peak) to 4.25% (end-2025), with 5.6% at current spot.

Gold as % of global household net wealth — 2000 to 2024

Gold mcap as % of global household net wealth · 2000–2024
Source: CS GWD 2010 • UBS GWD 2023 • UBS GWR 2024/2025

Household net wealth = financial + non-financial assets minus debt; Credit Suisse / UBS series (current exchange rates). Excludes bank balance sheets, so denominator is smaller and gold's share is higher (~2.7% end-2023; 3.2% end-2024) than against FSB total financial assets. Series begins 2000 — earliest year with consistent global estimates.
