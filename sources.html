Data sources

Every quantitative value on the dashboard is scripted from the primary sources below — no numbers are supplied by a model. Hover any figure in the dashboard for its specific source, year and method.

Rolling-returns & correlations tabs

Each series is reduced to an annual-average level index; the box-and-whisker plots overlapping N-year (5/10/20y) annualised total returns; the correlation matrix uses annual total returns. Real = deflated by Shiller annual-average CPI. Windows start ≥ 1901, end ≤ 2024. The Period slider clips the sample client-side (handles held ≥ 20 years apart).

Gold-holdings tabs (Snapshot & Trends)

Central-bank reserves

  • World Gold Council — Gold Reserves by Country (underlying IMF IFS). Quarterly tonnage, value, % of reserves, 2000-Q1+ (123 countries).
  • IMF — International Liquidity (IFS). Annual gold-reserve volume back to 1950 (187 countries + World aggregate) — drives the 50-year / Max trend windows.
  • World Bank — Commodity Price Data ("Pink Sheet"), annual gold price 1960+ ($35/oz flat 1950–59).
  • World Bank — GDP (current US$) for the % of GDP metric.

Private / household gold stock (estimates)

  • World Gold Council — above-ground stocks & market size (India ~25,000t; world private ≈ 144,000t).
  • Steinbeis University / Reisebank — Germany private stock survey (9,089t).
  • WGC US Gold Demand Trends + 10.8% ownership survey — US private-stock triangulation.
  • Italy (Kitco), Turkey (Türkiye Today / WGC), France & Switzerland (GoldBroker), Vietnam (National Assembly), China (analyst compilations).

Household wealth & income (context metrics)

  • UBS Global Wealth Databook — household wealth (% of wealth metric).
  • World Bank GNI per capita (Atlas) + OECD median disposable income.

Current gold spot: Yahoo Finance COMEX front-month (GC=F).
